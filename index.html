About the Tournament

Come for the golf.
Stay for the cause.

4-Person
Scramble

Grab your clubs and your crew — the 2027 Reach Charity Golf Tournament has a new look! Whether you're a scratch golfer or just there for the cart rides and good company, this is a day you won't want to miss.

The day features a 4-person scramble format suited to every skill level, along with lunch, a competitive round on a beautiful course, exciting prizes to win along the way, and an awards dinner to celebrate an incredible day for a wonderful cause.

The Cause

Every dollar goes
further than you think.

The Reach Charity Golf Tournament makes the Reach Conferences accessible to more students and leaders through scholarships and reduced costs.

Students are a generation rising. They need trusted adults, meaningful experiences, and the opportunity to discover purpose, build strong foundations, and see the difference their lives can make. Every dollar invested helps open that door — sending students and leaders home ready to lead with courage, serve with compassion, and bring real hope to the places they influence.
